FoodKart

An android application created with an objective to make ordering food more economical for students. This app pools enables individuals to pool together orders of various individuals together hence benefiting the individuals.


Project Repositories     Android App   Chrome Extension   Server
Technologies used: Android NodeJS Socket.IO Chrome Extension

Why?

We used to live in hostel during our engineering. And as usual mess food was not edible for most of the times. During those days we used to order food from outside. Now most of the time there were groups of 2-3 friends who used to order few items but most of the times we couldn't cross the minimum order amount requirements set by restuarants. So we used to go room to room asking if anyone wants to order from the same restuarant. This was a tiresome and frustrating thing to do everytime. So one day I had a thought - Why is there no app which could help me find such like minded people?

Hence foodkart was born. We worked for 36hours straight in an hackathon to build a prototype of the app.

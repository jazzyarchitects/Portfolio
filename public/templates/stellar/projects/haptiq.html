Haptiq

The prototype built during Hackfest 2017 in 36hrs, Haptiq is a fingerprint based authentication system for social logins. It enables you to login to various social sites using your Android Phone's Biometric authentication system. Your data is kept confidential. The app uses AES 256-bit encryption for communicating with the chrome extension.

Project Repositories     Android App   Chrome Extension   Server
Technologies used: Android NodeJS Socket.IO Chrome Extension

Why?

One day as usual I was browsing Facebook and then I went outside for a call. In the meantime my room mate opened my Facebook account on my laptop and then posted some embarrassing thing on my wall. Then from that day I used to log out every time I went away from laptop. But then one day he used my saved password to login to my account and do it again. Now I deleted all saved passwords and used to log out every time I went outside. But typing the password everytime was becoming a frustrating task.
I wondered if I could make something which would enable me to login to social sites without the need of password everytime in such a way that no one else can access it also...
